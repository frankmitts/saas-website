2. Do I keep all the rights to my logo?

Yes! You create your logo design, you have the rights to it. Generator is aimed at helping you maintain your brand, not to prevent you from your logo rights. If your subscription ends, you won’t be able to use Generator services or download your assets from your Brand Profile, but you can use your logo as you’d like.

3. How long is the subcription period?

Depends on what you’re looking for! We offer six, 12, and 24-month subscriptions. These can be canceled at any time and you can retain access through the remainder of the subscription period.

4. Can I just buy a logo without a subcription?

Your logo design is one part of your brand and we want to make it easy for you to expand, so we don’t offer any logo options without a subcription. Our Logo Only package offers unlimited logo storage, access to PNG and JPG file formats, and your logo for six, 12, or 24 months – all depending on the option that you choose!
